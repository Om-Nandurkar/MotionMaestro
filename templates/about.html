About Us

Our Mission

At Motion Maestro, we aim to empower individuals by creating an accessible and inclusive environment for Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing people. Our ASL detection project seeks to break down communication barriers through innovative technology that translates American Sign Language into readable text in real-time.

Our Technology

We leverage Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to accurately detect and interpret ASL gestures. By using hand-tracking modules and real-time prediction, our system ensures a smooth and reliable interaction for users.

Why It Matters

There are millions of people worldwide who rely on sign language to communicate. By developing this tool, we hope to make communication between signers and non-signers easier, whether in educational settings, workplaces, or everyday situations.
